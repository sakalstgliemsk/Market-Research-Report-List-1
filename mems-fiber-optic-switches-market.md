<p><p>MEMS fiber optic switches are critical components in optical communication, enabling efficient signal routing. MEMS single-mode optical switches are designed for applications using single-mode fibers, offering low loss and high bandwidth, ideal for long-distance communication. In contrast, MEMS multimode optical switches cater to multimode fibers, facilitating shorter-distance applications with higher data rates. Both types leverage MEMS technology for compactness and reliability, enhancing network performance and flexibility in various telecommunications and data center environments.</p></p>
