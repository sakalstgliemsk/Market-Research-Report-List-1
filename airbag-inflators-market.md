<p><strong>The Airbag Inflators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pyrotechnic Inflators</li><li>Stored Gas Inflators</li><li>Hybrid Inflators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airbag inflators market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Pyrotechnic Inflators**: These use a chemical reaction to rapidly produce gas and inflate airbags during a crash. </p><p>2. **Stored Gas Inflators**: These rely on compressed gases stored in a cylinder, releasing them to inflate airbags on impact. </p><p>3. **Hybrid Inflators**: Combining both pyrotechnic and stored gas technologies, hybrid inflators offer improved reliability and performance, providing a faster and more controlled inflation process in various crash scenarios.</p></p>
