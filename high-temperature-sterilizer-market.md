Steam sterilizers utilize high-pressure steam to eliminate microorganisms, making them ideal for heat-resistant items. Dry sterilizers employ dry heat to achieve sterilization, suitable for materials that may corrode or are sensitive to moisture. Each type is designed to ensure effective and safe sterilization of diverse materials.</p></p>
