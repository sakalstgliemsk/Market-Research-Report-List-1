<p><ul><li>Square Chisel Mortiser</li><li>Hollow Chisel Mortiser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mortiser market consists primarily of two types: the square chisel mortiser and the hollow chisel mortiser. Square chisel mortisers are designed to cut precise, square holes for traditional joinery, making them popular in woodworking. Hollow chisel mortisers, on the other hand, utilize a cylindrical chisel, allowing for clean mortise cuts while accommodating larger or more complex joints. Both types serve specialized functions in carpentry and furniture making, with varying features that cater to different craftsmanship needs and preferences.</p></p>
