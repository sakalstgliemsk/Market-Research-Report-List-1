<p><strong>The Recycled (Retreaded) Tyres Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre Cure</li><li>Mold Cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recycled (retreaded) tire market primarily consists of two types: pre-cure and mold cure. Pre-cure retreading involves applying a pre-manufactured tread rubber to the tire casing, allowing for a more flexible design and various tread patterns. Conversely, mold cure retreading uses a heated mold to bond new tread material to the existing tire, providing a uniform tread pattern and enhanced durability. Both methods offer eco-friendly alternatives to new tires, reducing waste and lowering costs for consumers.</p></p>
