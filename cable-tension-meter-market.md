<p><p>The cable tension meter market consists of three primary types: handheld, hanging, and vertical. Handheld meters are portable devices designed for easy use in various settings, allowing users to measure cable tension quickly. Hanging meters are typically suspended from the cable, providing accurate readings without direct contact. Vertical meters are installed along a vertical cable path, offering continuous tension monitoring and data logging capabilities. Each type caters to specific operational needs across industries such as construction, telecommunications, and entertainment.</p></p>
