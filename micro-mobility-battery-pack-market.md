<p><p>The Micro Mobility Battery Pack market includes two primary types: Lead-Acid Battery Packs and Lithium-Ion Battery Packs. Lead-acid batteries are cost-effective and commonly used in conventional applications but are heavier and have a shorter life cycle. Conversely, Lithium-Ion Battery Packs are lighter, offer higher energy density, longer life spans, and faster charging times, making them ideal for modern micro-mobility solutions like e-scooters and e-bikes. The choice between these types influences performance, cost, and environmental impact.</p></p>
