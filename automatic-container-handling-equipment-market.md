<p><p>The Automatic Container Handling Equipment market encompasses several key types. Automated Stacking Cranes (ASCs) efficiently stack containers in storage yards. Rubber Tired Gantry (RTG) cranes transport and position containers within container terminals, offering flexibility. Ship-to-Shore (STS) cranes handle container loading and unloading between ships and the terminal. Automatic Guided Vehicles (AGVs) autonomously transport containers within facilities. Additionally, other equipment includes various automation technologies enhancing container movement and handling, contributing to improved efficiency and reduced operational costs in logistics.</p></p>
