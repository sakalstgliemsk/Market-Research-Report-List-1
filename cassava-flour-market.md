<p><ul><li>Sweet Cassava Flour</li><li>Bitter Cassava Flour</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cassava flour market is divided into two main types: sweet cassava flour and bitter cassava flour. Sweet cassava flour, derived from the sweet variety of cassava, is primarily used in baking and cooking due to its pleasant taste and versatility. In contrast, bitter cassava flour comes from the bitter variety, which contains higher levels of cyanogenic glycosides. This type is processed to reduce toxins and is often used in traditional dishes and local markets. Each type serves distinct culinary purposes and consumer preferences.</p></p>
