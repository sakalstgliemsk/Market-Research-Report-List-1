<p><ul><li>Taper Shank</li><li>Straight Handle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The T-Slot Cutters market is categorized primarily into two types: taper shank and straight handle cutters. Taper shank cutters feature a conical connection, providing enhanced stability and rigidity during machining. They are often preferred for high-speed applications. Straight handle cutters, on the other hand, have a uniform diameter and are simpler to handle, making them ideal for general-purpose tasks. Both types serve essential roles in creating T-slots for securing workpieces in various machining processes, catering to different user needs.</p></p>
