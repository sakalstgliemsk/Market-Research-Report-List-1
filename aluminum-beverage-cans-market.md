<p><p>The aluminum beverage cans market comprises two primary types: three-piece cans and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans are crafted from three separate aluminum components—two bodies and a lid—which are welded together. They are commonly used for products like soups and some beverages. In contrast, two-piece cans are made from a single aluminum sheet that is drawn and ironed into a cylindrical shape, providing a seamless structure that enhances durability and reduces material usage, typically favored for soft drinks and beer.</p></p>
